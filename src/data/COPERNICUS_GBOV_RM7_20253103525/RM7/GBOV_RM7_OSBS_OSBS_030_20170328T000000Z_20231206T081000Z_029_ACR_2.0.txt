Site_Description=Ordway-Swisher Biological Station (OSBS) is located approximately 30 km east of Gainesville (Florida). It is operated by the University of Florida and comprises over 3,700 ha. Ordway-Swisher is dominated by pine and turkey oak (Quercus laevis Walter) vegetation with a grass and forb groundcover. Mean canopy height is approximately 23 meters. The forest is maintained by fire and has a relatively open structure: it is managed with prescribed burns at a frequency of 3-4 years.
